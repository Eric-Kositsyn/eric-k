A bit about me

I'm Eric Kositsyn, a high school student and robotics engineer with a passion for designing and building things that solve real problems. My interests span mechanical design, software development, artificial intelligence, and manufacturing, and I'm always looking for new ways to turn ideas into reality.

My goal is to build innovative engineering projects, create products that make an impact, and continue learning as an engineer.
